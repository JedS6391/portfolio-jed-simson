About

Me

I am currently a student at the University of Waikato, studying towards a Bachelor of Computing and Mathematical Sciences (Hons). I have a strong interest in artificial intelligence, computer networks and communications, and compiler theory/construction.

Whilst studying I have maintained grades in the A range and have worked on a variety of personal side projects which can be viewed on my GitLab account. One of my favourite projects was a website I created to provide an alternative, and more reliable interface for searching reddit, which can be found here.
